These screenshots together showcase the complete functionality of the Power BI Online Retail Dashboard. The main visuals page highlights high-level sales trends through charts like "Sales by Country" and "Monthly Sales," along with interactive slicers and a product-wise transaction table for detailed exploration. The tooltip page demonstrates the use of custom tooltips that appear when hovering over data points, offering invoice-level details in a compact format. The drillthrough pane provides deeper customer-level insights with metrics such as average quantity, total spend, and total orders, enabling users to analyze individual purchase behaviors in context.
